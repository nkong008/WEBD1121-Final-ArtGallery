OMARI MARTINEZ

ARTIST BIOGRAPHY

Omari Martinez is an internationally renowned photographer
with over 7 years’ of experience in shooting documentaries and
editorial campaigns. Omari assignments have taken him across
Africa, Europe, the Middle East, Asia and the U.S.


He holds an undergraduate and master’s degree in Engineering
from Imperial College, London, England. Omari cureently resides
in Brooklyn, New York.

Keen to share his extensive expertise with the next generation,
Omari hosts photography workshops around the world and runs
various mentoring programs in West Africa for photography students.
