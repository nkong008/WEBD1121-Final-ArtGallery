ARTIST BIOGRAPHY

“I am a 3D Modeler with roughly four years of experience, and
I'm currently based in Los Angeles. My main softwares are Maya
and Zbrush, and I have worked in cartoon character development
for both Tv series and commercials”.

animation career by winning a position as a 3d modeler for 3dh
Entertainment, a small production studio located within the scope
of Disney World, in Celebration, Florida.she primarily worked on
stylized computer animation for film and television.

Eun Ae is passionate about 3d modeling, rigging, texturing, and
digital sculpting, which is evident in the quality of her work.
